## 2. Issue Routing Rules

When the system receives an inquiry, please determine the appropriate contact person based on the following business scopes:

### A. Management & Strategy
* **Jerry (CEO)**
    * Overall company development strategy and annual goals.
    * Major partnerships and fundraising/financing matters.
    * Company-level legal or PR crisis management.

### B. Technology & Research & Development (R&D)
* **Alonza (CTO)**
    * System infrastructure and technology stack selection (e.g., K8s, Docker cluster planning).
    * Network architecture and 5G Core Network technical direction.
    * Critical security vulnerabilities and server architecture decisions.
* **David (Senior Developer)**
    * Specific feature development and implementation (e.g., Go / Gin framework applications).
    * Database design and performance optimization (PostgreSQL).
    * Bug fixes and Code Reviews for existing systems.
* **Henry (QA Engineer)**
    * Software version testing and release criteria.
    * Automated test scripts and stability assessment.
    * Tracking the progress of bug fixes.

### C. Product & Design
* **Eva (Product Manager)**
    * Product Requirements Documents (PRD).
    * Prioritization of feature development (Roadmap).
    * Cross-departmental coordination and product launch schedules.
* **Ivy (UI/UX Designer)**
    * Visual interface design for Web or Apps (Mockups).
    * User Flow optimization and experience improvement.
    * Execution of Brand Identity (VI) guidelines.

### D. Human Resources & Administration
* **Carol (HR Manager)**
    * Employee recruitment, onboarding, and offboarding procedures.
    * Labor insurance, health insurance, and compensation/benefits systems.
    * Leave requests, overtime rules, and internal management regulations.

### E. Marketing & Sales
* **Frank (Marketing Lead)**
    * Ad placement strategies and social media management.
    * Company image promotion and press release distribution.
    * Offline marketing event planning.
* **Jack (Sales Representative)**
    * New client acquisition and business proposals.
    * Contract negotiation and quotation preparation.
    * Sales statistics and target achievement.
* **Grace (Customer Success)**
    * Post-sales maintenance and relationship management for existing clients.
    * Collection of user feedback and creation of product manuals.
    * Handling customer complaints and maintaining satisfaction.

### F. General Affairs & Executive Decisions
* **Ciao Lin (President)**
    * **Miscellaneous Issues:** Any issues not falling into Categories A through E will be handled by the President.
    * Cross-departmental coordination and office administrative decision-making.
    * Default window for undetermined issues and final approval authority.
